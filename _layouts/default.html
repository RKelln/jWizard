About jWizard

jWizard is a user-friendly, flexible and slim wizard plugin for the jQuery Javascript Framework, built atop the jQuery UI Widget Factory.

Based off of the Windows wizards as well as the .NET Wizard Control, it is meant to enrich your user interface with a wizard that helps guide your users through a series of steps, reducing a large and complicated process into small chunks to keep your users from being overwhelmed.

I've included a number of demonstrations of the functionality and customizability of jWizard here. Feel free to contact me with further questions.
